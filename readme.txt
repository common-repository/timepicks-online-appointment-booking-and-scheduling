####How It Works####

- Clients visit your site and click "Book Online"
- The visitor is then taken through a step by step scheduling wizard to pick the desired
service or appointment type offered by your business.
- Visitor browses available openings in real-time and selects the desired time.
- Client then enters their information and register an account with your business to
speed up the process for future bookings made online.
- Immediate appointment notifications are sent to you and a confirmation email is sent to
the client.
- Email reminders are automatically sent to clients prioar to their appointment.

== Installation ==

### Installation via the Plugin Manager directly ###

1. Goto 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Goto 'Add New'
1. Search for "TimePicks"

### Installation via uploading the zipfile into WordPress ###

1. Download the zipfile 
1. Goto 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Goto 'Add New'
1. Click 'Upload' below the Installed Plugins menu
1. Choose the zipfile you downloaded and click 'Install Now'
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

### Install via manual upload (FTP/SSH/etc) ###

1. Download and extract the zipfile
1. Upload the resultant directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
